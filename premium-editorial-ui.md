## Goal

Produce interfaces that feel handcrafted instead of AI generated.

The final product should resemble premium websites such as:

- Apple
- Aesop
- Zara Home
- Studio McGee
- Restoration Hardware
- Notion
- Vercel
- Linear

---

## Visual Language

Prioritize

- Spacious layouts
- Elegant typography
- Editorial composition
- Luxury aesthetics
- Soft colors
- Large imagery
- Natural photography
- Minimal distractions

Never create cluttered dashboards.

---

## Typography

Preferred fonts

Headings

- Cormorant Garamond
- Playfair Display
- Bodoni Moda

Accent

- Allura
- Great Vibes

Body

- Inter

Use typography hierarchy instead of excessive colors.

---

Use warm neutral palettes.

Example

Background

#F8F5F1

Surface

#FFFFFF

Primary

#2E2B29

Secondary

#666666

Accent

#C5A07A

Borders

#E8E3DD

Avoid saturated colors unless requested.

---

## Layout

Prefer

Large whitespace

Editorial sections

Split layouts

Large hero images

Offset cards

Image overlays

Floating content blocks

Soft borders

Rounded corners

Avoid cramped layouts.

---

## Components

Design reusable components.

Examples

Hero

Section Title

Editorial Card

Image Grid

Feature Block

Testimonial

CTA

Gallery

Navigation

Footer

Each component should share spacing, typography, shadows and radius.

---

## Images

Images should

Fill the available space

Have soft shadows

Use subtle rounded corners

Support overlapping compositions

Never stretch.

---

## Buttons

Buttons should feel refined.

Use

Thin borders

Subtle hover animations

Small elevation

Elegant typography

No oversized buttons.

---

## Animation

Use tasteful motion.

Examples

Fade

Slide

Scale

Parallax

Reveal on scroll

Soft hover lift

Avoid excessive animations.

---

## Accessibility

Maintain WCAG AA contrast.

Keyboard navigation.

Visible focus states.

Semantic HTML.

Screen-reader support.

---

## Responsiveness

Mobile-first.

Preserve spacing.

Do not stack everything immediately.

Respect editorial layouts.
